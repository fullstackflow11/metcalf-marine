The Technology

Two paths to a clean burn.

Modern DPF technology has drastically lowered the exhaust temperatures needed for regeneration. Our experts determine which system fits each generator on your vessel.

Passive Regeneration

Uses only existing exhaust heat

For engines that create sufficient heat, we can now offer units completely without the heating elements used in older technology — regeneration happens on the exhaust heat the engine already produces.

The cleanest, simplest install where the powerplant supports it.

Best when — the generator runs hot enough to burn off particulate on its own.
Active Regeneration

Engineered assist for cooler engines

Some generators are better served by an active regeneration system. Our experts help you determine which system is best and explain the differences and advantages of each technology.

Every unit is custom engineered and designed for your specific vessel.

Best when — exhaust temperatures alone won't sustain regeneration.
