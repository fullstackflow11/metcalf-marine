The Standard

Water-jacketed parts are always purge welded.

Marine exhaust lives in the hardest environment on any vessel — salt, heat cycling, vibration and condensate acids, all at once. The first thing to go is almost always the weld.

So we purge weld every water-jacketed part — shielding the weld's back side during fabrication to ensure its integrity, in 316L stainless (or AL6XN on request). Every order gets in-process and final quality inspection before it leaves the shop.

Build it once, in the right alloy, welded right — and it outlasts the vessel it's fitted to.
